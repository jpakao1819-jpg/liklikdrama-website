About Us

Our Story

From a vision to empower PNG communities

The Beginning

LiklikDrama was born from a simple observation: PNG's local businesses and storytellers needed a platform that truly understood their culture and community. We saw small shops struggling to reach customers, and talented storytellers with no outlet for their voices.

We decided to bridge this gap by creating a platform that combines modern marketing techniques with authentic PNG cultural values.

Our Growth

What started as a small initiative has grown into a movement. Today, we've helped over 100 local businesses increase their visibility, published stories from 50+ PNG writers, and built a community of 10,000+ followers across social media.

But we're just getting started. Our vision is to become PNG's premier platform for cultural media and business promotion.
