About LiklikDrama

Preserving Cultural Heritage Through Premium Storytelling

◈ Our Story

LiklikDrama Culture Sphere was founded with a vision to preserve and celebrate PNG's rich cultural heritage through the power of digital storytelling. We are committed to bringing traditional narratives, performances, and artistic expressions to the modern world while maintaining their authentic essence.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a platform where cultural stories could be shared, preserved, and passed down to future generations in their purest form.

◈ Our Mission

To be the premier platform for cultural storytelling, providing world-class production services and interactive experiences that celebrate, preserve, and promote Papua New Guinea's diverse cultural heritage.

We strive to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern technology, ensuring that cultural knowledge remains accessible and relevant for generations to come.

◈ Our Vision

To become the global benchmark for cultural digital platforms, showcasing Papua New Guinea's rich traditions to the world while empowering local communities to share their stories with pride and authenticity.

We envision a future where every cultural story has a platform, every artist has a voice, and every tradition finds its place in the digital age.

◈ Our Values

  • 🎭 Cultural Authenticity: Preserving the true essence of traditions
  • 🌟 Excellence: World-class production quality
  • 🤝 Community: Building strong cultural connections
  • 🎨 Innovation: Blending tradition with technology
  • 📚 Education: Learning through cultural storytelling
  • 💎 Premium Quality: Delivering exceptional experiences

◈ Our Team

Our team consists of passionate cultural enthusiasts, professional storytellers, technical experts, and community leaders who share a common goal: to preserve and celebrate PNG's cultural heritage.

We bring together diverse expertise in filmmaking, digital media, cultural studies, and community engagement to create comprehensive cultural experiences.

  • 🎬 Over 100 cultural productions completed
  • 🤝 Partnerships with 50+ cultural communities
  • 📺 National television features
  • 🌐 Global digital reach
  • 🎓 Educational program development
